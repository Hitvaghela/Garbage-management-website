Plastic Management Service

We are committed to managing waste in an eco-friendly manner. Our mission is to reduce landfill waste and promote recycling and composting.




OBTAINING A VOUCHER

We're committed to eco-friendly waste management. You can earn vouchers by showing us your trash bill. It doesn't matter if you do it monthly or yearly. The weight of your recyclables determines your vouchers.

When you provide your plastic waste daily, we generate a trash bill for you. This bill not only records your daily contributions but also helps us identify the weight of your plastic waste. By keeping track of this weight, we determine your environmental impact and rewards in our voucher system.



Voucher System

Voucher 1

If your plastic contribution is between 1 kg and 5 kg, you will receive Voucher 1, which can be redeemed for a discount on our services.

Discount Details:

- Get a 5% discount on your choice of product

- Get Free Bus Pass for 1 month

- 5 paper bag

Voucher 2

If your plastic contribution is between 5 kg and 10 kg, you will receive Voucher 2, which can be redeemed for exclusive eco-friendly products.

Product Options:

- Get a 10% discount on your choice of product

- Get Free Bus Pass for 3 month

- Eco-friendly utensil sets

- 10 Paper bag
